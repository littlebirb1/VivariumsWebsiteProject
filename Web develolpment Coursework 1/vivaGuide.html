STEP 1: Flooring

To make your home vivarium, start out by gathering materials and choosing a container or tank for you. Then, add suitable drainage layer at the bottom of your vivarium and a false bottom floor above it.

STEP 2: More Flooring

Moving on, Add a fabric layer over the "false bottom". Then, add a suitable subtrate layer with optional cushioning in the form of "leaf litter".

STEP 3: Living Conditions

After that, establish the moisture, temperature, light and ventillation/filtration systems of the vivarium for the desired animals for the vivarium. Don't forget to decotarations that suit the animal's environment.

STEP 4: Plantlife

Add an appropriate amount of plantlife into the vivarium.

STEP 5: The Animals

Place your pets into the vivarium and enjoy!

Tip: Don't overdose your terrarium with necessities, such as water and sunglight. Keeping them to a medium is more sustainable.
