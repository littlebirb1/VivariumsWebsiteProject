Aquariums

A type of a Vivarium that is fully Aquatic, serving as a home for aquatic animals like fish and some Crustaceans. Its design can vary from individual but usually includes a full tank of water, a sandy or rocky floor, and some vegetation to decorate or to serve as food for the fauna.

Terrariums

A type of Vivarium that is partially aquatic and terrestrial, serving as a home for animals such as arthropods, reptiles, and even smaller mammals. Its environment can be customized to suit the main species, whether by adding more vegetation and plant life or more rocks and stony obstacles.

Desertariums

A type of Vivarium that is fully terrestrial, designed for animals familiar with sandy or arid environments. It serves as a habitat for arachnids, lizards, and others, with minimal vegetation and plant life.

Paludariums

A type of Vivarium that is semi-aquatic and terrestrial, simulating river or wet environments. Amphibians, fish, and wet arthropods thrive in these types of vivariums.
